Story

Yi-Cha is a place for all tea lovers ! Yi-Cha means Japanese tea ceremony and it's ART of tea!

Beyond just serving and receiving tea, one of the main purposes of the tea ceremony is for the guests
to enjoy the hospitality of the host in an atmosphere distinct from the fast pace of everyday life.

Signature Drinks

First signature is Matcha Frappucino. Our Matcha is used in the Japanese tea ceremony. The taste
of high grade Matcha is smooth and mellow, and not at all bitter. Unique rich foamy texture. High grade
Matcha is a bright color of green.

Our Second signature is the Brown Sugar Boba Milk. our boba from handrolled tapioca dough and cook
them until they’re perfectly soft and chewy. Then we stir-fry brown sugar until it’s toasted and caramelized
and giving off that unmistakable aroma.
